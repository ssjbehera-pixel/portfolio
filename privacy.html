Privacy Policy

Effective date:

1) Who we are

CloudInfraFlow (“we”, “us”) provides cloud engineering and platform services.

2) What we collect

  • Contact: name, email, phone when you reach out.
  • Business: company, project details you share for estimates.
  • Operational: logs/metrics strictly for support if engaged.
  • Site analytics: basic telemetry to improve the site; no sensitive data.

3) How we use data

  • Respond to inquiries, provide quotes, deliver services, and support.
  • Improve reliability, security, and usability.
  • Meet legal, compliance, and audit obligations.

4) Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share with vetted sub-processors only to deliver services (e.g., cloud providers, CI/CD, ticketing). Contracts include confidentiality and data-protection terms.

5) Security

Least-privilege access, encryption in transit/at rest where applicable, secrets management, and audit logging. Concerns? Report an issue.

6) Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for its purpose or as required by law/contract. You may request deletion where applicable.

7) Your rights

Subject to local law, you may request access, correction, portability, or deletion of your personal data. We’ll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8) Cookies & analytics

We use minimal cookies/analytics for performance and troubleshooting. You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

9) International transfers

Data may be processed in regions where we or sub-processors operate, with appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs or equivalent mechanisms).

10) Children

Our services are business-focused and not directed to children under 16.

11) Contact

Questions or requests: hello@cloudinfraflow.com.
