Popular FAQs

How do I get a project estimate?

Use the Contact page and describe goals, timelines, cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP), compliance (HIPAA/ISO), and traffic scale. We’ll reply with a short architecture outline and quote.

Which clouds and tools do you support?

AWS, Azure, GCP, and Vultr. Tooling includes Kubernetes (EKS/AKS/GKE), Terraform, Argo CD, Jenkins, Docker, Git/GitHub, Prometheus/Grafana, and ELK. See Cloud Providers.

Do you help with compliance (HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001)?

Yes. We design landing zones, network isolation, IAM, encryption, logging, and access controls mapped to your compliance framework and audit scope.

What is your typical engagement model?

We start with discovery + design, then implement with GitOps/CI-CD and SRE runbooks. Knowledge transfer and documentation are included.

How do I report a security issue or fraud?

Email hello@cloudinfraflow.com with subject “SECURITY” or visit Fraud alert and Report issue.
